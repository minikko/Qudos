Our Story

How it all started in 2016?

Qudos Technologies, Inc. (the “Company”) was formed on September 20, 2016, as a New York corporation and converted to a Delaware corporation on December 13, 2016.

The Company is in the business of technology and has developed a unique ride-hailing mobile application. Its principal offices are presently located at 19-69 Steinway St 2nd Floor, Long Island City, NY 11105.

Mission

Qudos aims to make ride-hailing personal by facilitating a connection between riders and drivers while providing a platform that caters to their needs.

Vision

Create a community of loyal riders and drivers that delivers a VIP ride hailing experience.

Jerry Raio
COO, DIRECTOR & INVESTOR RELATIONS

Jerry is the Founder, President and CEO of Arbor Lane Advisors, Inc., a financial service focused consulting firm with expertise in product development, product management, marketing and distribution of investment products. His career on Wall Street spans over 30 years, mostly focused on the distribution of Equity Capital Markets product to Financial Advisors and retail investors. Thomas Sweeney
DIRECTOR

Alexander Sklavos
Director & General Counsel

Alex is admitted to the New York State Bar and the United States Federal Courts, and the District of Columbia. Mr. Sklavos received a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics from Union College and his Juris Doctor from St. John’s University School of Law where he received academic honors, and was declared the Clinician of the year award for his work with disadvantaged senior citizens. Mr. Sklavos was formerly a clerk to Justice Thomas Demakos of the New York State Supreme Court. He is a former adjunct professor of Touro College, instructing in Business Law and Real Estate. He is currently a member of the New York State Bar Association and the Nassau County Bar Association, where he formerly sat on the Board of Directors. Mr. Sklavos’ emphasis is in Commercial Litigation, Corporate Litigation, Real Estate Litigation, Real Estate and Family Law.

Christian Palmer
