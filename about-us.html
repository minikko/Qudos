Our Story

How it all started in 2016?

Qudos Technologies, Inc. (the “Company”) was formed on September 20, 2016, as a New York corporation and converted to a Delaware corporation on December 13, 2016.

The Company is in the business of technology and has developed a unique ride-hailing mobile application. Its principal offices are presently located at 19-69 Steinway St 2nd Floor, Long Island City, NY 11105.

Mission

Qudos aims to make ride-hailing personal by facilitating a connection between riders and drivers while providing a platform that caters to their needs.

Vision

Create a community of loyal riders and drivers that delivers a VIP ride hailing experience.
