There is a pending charge on my account, what does this mean?

Everytime a payment method on your Qudos account is newly added or unutilized for 15 days, Qudos will verify it by proceeding with a temporary authorization hold.

Although you may take notice of authorization holds as amounts listed on your account as pending, these small amounts are never literally charged to your account.

Qudos may place a temporary authorization hold for the beforehand payment of the trip on your payment method at the start of a trip. This will reflect as a "pending" charge on your account's payment method. This authorization hold is then converted to a charge for the final trip payment when the trip is completed.

Depending on your bank policy, the original authorization hold should be invalidated from your payment method within 15 business days if the trip has been canceled or if the total fare is different than the beforehand price in your app. Please contact your bank at first hand if you would like to confirm a particular item on your statement.
