Start Driving

Welcome to your Qudos Driving Journey!

Here are some useful tips and FAQs to help you make the most of your driving experience.

Before starting the ride, always verify your rider’s name and destination.

Say a quick introduction, such as “Hi! I’m (name), your Qudos Driver.”

Provide the best driving experience by making riders feel comfortable. You can ask questions like “Is the temperature okay?” or “Would you like to listen to something on the radio?”


After the ride, encourage the rider to add you as their QudosFave driver so they can book you again. We suggest saying:

“Thank you for riding with me, it would be a pleasure to drive you again. If you liked your ride and would like to schedule a future ride with me, you can book me as a favorite driver.”

Be sure to mention promo codes such as:

  • rideQudos: 20% off your first ride for up to $25
  • QudosFave: 25% off your first ride for up to $25

Discounts are applicable on their 1st, 5th, and 10th ride.
