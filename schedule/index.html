How to schedule rides with QudosSchedule℠

After setting your destination, a pop up menu will be displayed. Tap the QudosSchedule icon to proceed.

Set the date and time for your scheduled pickup. You can schedule a ride up to one month. Tap Set to proceed.

Select batch or a specific driver from your QudosFave.

How to check your currently scheduled rides

Tap the Qudos Menu icon, then tap on Scheduled Rides.

Scroll through the dates to view your scheduled rides.
