Qudos Referral Program

Start referring Qudos to friends and family to get awesome discounts. Here’s how!

Qudos offers bonuses to riders for every successful referral.

Here’s how to get your referral code.

For mobile, tap the Menu bar to open the navigation drawer.

For web, click the dropdown button beside your name, then click My Profile.

From the navigation drawer, tap Invite & Get Free Rides to view your referral code.

Under Other Information section shows your invite code.

Tap or click the invite button to automatically copy your Invite Link to your clipboard.

Awesome! Now you’re set. Simply paste the link to any messaging or social media app to share your referral link.

When your family and friends use your link and sign up for Qudos, you get an awesome referral bonus!

Use promo code rideQudos and get up to 30% off your first 10 rides, capped at $10. Terms apply.
