What is QudosList?

QudosList is a social network for riders and drivers to connect.

How to post on QudosList

How to post on QudosList

Tap the text box that says “Want to share something?” in the description.

You can share thoughts with your friends and express your appreciation towards a specific driver or to Qudos’ services.

Tap the Post button.

Great! Now you have shared your first post. Your added friends and family will get to see your previous and future posts by scrolling through the news feed or visiting your profile.

How to share your driver on QudosList

How to share your driver on QudosList

From the navigation bar, tap the Users icon, then tap QudosFave to display a list of QudosFave drivers.

Tap MyQudos to display your own set of QudosFave drivers, and click the Share button.

Awesome! You have shared your first driver to your QudosList.

Share your thoughts about the recent ride with a fave, put in a good review for your friends and family to see.

You are all set! We'll send you reminders the day of the trip and notify you when your ride is on its way.

How to know more about your driver

How to know more about
your driver

You can search your driver’s name from the search bar then tap on the result to
visit their profile.

From here, you can choose to add them as your QudosFave, or follow or share their profile.
