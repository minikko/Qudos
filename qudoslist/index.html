How to post on QudosListSM

Tap the text box that says “Want to share something?”

You can share updates with your friends and express your appreciation towards a specific Qudos driver or to Qudos’ services.

Tap the Post button.

Great! Now you have shared your first post. Your added friends and family will get to see your posts by scrolling through the news feed or visiting your profile.

How to share your driver

From the navigation bar, tap the Users icon, then tap QudosFave to display a list of QudosFave drivers.

Tap MyQudos to display your own set of QudosFave drivers, and tap the Share button.

Awesome! You have shared your first driver to your QudosListSM.

Share your thoughts about your ride and put in a good word for friends and family.

How to know more about your driver

Use the search bar to find the name of your driver. Visit their profile to get to know them, the features they offer, and view ratings.

You can also add them as a QudosFave as well as follow and share their profile with other riders.
