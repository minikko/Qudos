Referral Program

Start referring Qudos to friends and family to get awesome discounts. Qudos offers bonuses to riders for every successful referral. Here’s how!

How To Get Your Referral Code

For mobile:

  • From the home screen, tap the Menu bar to open the navigation bar.
  • Tap Invite & Get Free Rides to view your referral code.
  • Tap the invite button to automatically copy your Invite Link to your clipboard.

For tablet & desktop:

  • From your dashboard, click the dropdown button beside your name.
  • Click My Profile to show your account’s general information.
  • Click the invite button to automatically copy your Invite Link to your clipboard.
