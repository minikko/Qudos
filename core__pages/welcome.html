 Welcome to your Qudos Driving Journey

Here are some useful tips to help you make the most of your driving experience.

Quick introduction

Before starting the ride, always verify your rider’s name and destination. Say a quick introduction.

“Hi! I am (name), your Qudos Driver.”

Small talk

Provide the best riding experience by making riders feel comfortable. You can ask questions like:

“Thank you for riding with me, it would be a pleasure to drive you again. If you liked your ride and would like to schedule a future ride with me, you can book me as a favorite driver.”

Open possibility for future ride

After the ride, encourage the rider to add you as their QudosFave driver so they can book you again. We suggest saying:

“Is the temperature okay?”

“Would you like to listen to something on the radio?”
