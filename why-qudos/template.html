About Qudos

Qudos is the only ride-sharing app that connects riders to drivers they like and trust - again and again.


In a Nutshell


Unlike other ride-hailing apps, Qudos enables riders and drivers to closely interact. The app allows for riders, who particularly like the services of a certain driver, to request for the same driver again and again - ensuring trust and reliability. Such a feature not only serves the rider, but also helps the driver build a loyal, steady customer base. An interactive relationship between the rider and driver is a win-win situation for everyone.

Apart from getting rides from the ride hailing public, Qudos has corporate accounts that includes medical offices, small coporations, local hotels and gyms. These corporate accounts are a more targeted and reliable source of repeat rides for our drivers.

Qudos provides a platform for our drivers and riders to interact repeatedly. Through this, we empower our drivers to directly connect with and develop their own set of repeat customers, not only with individual riders but with corporate accounts such as medical offices, hotels, corporations, etc.

Frequently Asked Questions

  What is QUDOS?

  What is Qudos' rate per mile? rate per minute?

Qudos rates are almost the same with Uber however, Qudos gives our drivers up to 80% of the fare, so you end up earning more compared to Uber/Lyft.

  Why will I switch to Qudos when I can get more riders thru Uber/Lyft/other apps?

You don’t actually have to switch. Qudos is designed to be an additional source of income. You can continue to be an Uber/Lyft driver but keep your Qudos app open in the background. When there is a ride request, you will get an SMS telling you that you have a rider request, this way, you will never miss a ride request from Qudos.

What is special about Qudos is our QudosFave™ feature. If a passenger likes you, they can save you as a favorite driver in their QudosFave list, and pick you everytime they request a ride near wherever you are.

  What does it mean to be a QudosFave driver?

It means that as long as you are within the rider’s request radius, the rider can always request you as their driver.

This also allows the rider to schedule their rides, specifically with you whether they are going to the airport, doctor’s appointment etc.

  Tell me about your Book Later Feature

This feature allows riders to schedule rides with anyone in their QudosFave list. For example, they can schedule rides next week. As soon as the ride is booked, both riders and drivers know their driver, car make, route and fare price. Drivers will also get constant notifications to keep on reminding you of your ride on the day of the scheduled ride request.

  What are other features of Qudos?

Our technology allows us to tag our drivers with certain affiliations or traits. For example, we can tag a driver as “Spanish Speaking” or “Rotary Club”, so that a rider, who can only speak Spanish, can choose a driver they are comfortable with. By having an affiliation such as Rotary Club, riders can pick a driver who is a Rotary member and support causes that are close to their hearts.

  How do I sign up to be a Qudos Driver?

It is so easy! First, go to www.rideQudos.com. Click “start driving” and then follow the easy sign up directions. The whole process can be done thru your mobile phone.

You will need to upload a photo of the ff documents:

  • Driver’s License
  • TL&C Driver’s License
  • Car Registration
  • TL&C Inspection Report or Diamond Sticker
  • Certificate of Liability Insurance
  • For Hire Vehicle Insurance
  • Profile Picture

    • You will also have to fill in the bank account details.

    If your documents and bank details are clear and complete, the Qudos Headquarters can review and approve your registration within 24 hours (Mon-Fri). Once approved, you will receive a link via SMS to download the Qudos Partner App. Download and install the app, and you’re good to go!

      Where can I get my payouts? How to add my bank info to receive payout?

    Login to you driver portal at www.rideQudos.com and fill in your bank details (where your earnings will be credited). Qudos does not have access to your bank details. Stripe, a 3rd party service, securely keeps all your bank information.

      When do I get paid?

    Qudos works on a Monday to Sunday weekly billing schedule and payments are credited to your account every Thursday. So, you will get paid for all the rides you made from Monday to Sunday, on the upcoming Thursday.
