RIDER CANCELLATION POLICY

After a ride has been booked, you can cancel it by selecting the "Cancel Ride" option, at the bottom of your phone screen. Our cancellation fees vary by vehicle class and is as follows:
QS & QLE Any rides cancelled more than 2 minutes after the driver accepts reserves the right to charge a $5 cancellation fee.
LUXE, GRANDE, QXL Any rides cancelled more than 2 minutes after the driver accepts reserves the right to charge a $10 cancellation fee.
ELITE Any Rides Cancelled more than 2 minutes after the driver accepts reserves the right to charge a $30 cancellation fee.
These fees are set in place to compensate the driver for gas and any inconvenience they may face.

DAMAGE & CLEANING POLICY

Qudos reserves the right to charge riders the cost of rectifying damage caused by the deliberate, negligent, or reckless act of the rider to the driver’s vehicle. Should this damage come to light after the rider has departed, we reserve the right to make a charge to the rider’s card. We will, however, make every effort to asses any damage and carry out a thorough inspection of the vehicle by asking the driver to submit photos and a description of the vehicle within one hour of the ride.

Each driver must submit the following within one hour after the passenger has left.
• A detailed description of the mess
• 3-5 photos of the mess
• The riders name and full trip details

Qudos damage fees range between $10 to $100 and there are 3 levels of severity that you can be charged with.
$100: This is the highest amount a driver can request. This amount is charged if you are responsible for major bodily fluid messes inside the driver’s car. This includes vomit, urine, blood, etc…
$50: This is the median surcharge for messes.
$10-$40: This is the basic surcharge and it covers all situations from small food spills, left behind trash, dirt or sand, menial messes, and etc.

How will I know if I was charged a cleaning fee?
Qudos will send you a thorough email with photographic proof of the mess you created as well as an account of the trip.

How to dispute a cleaning or damage fee.
If you believe that you are being wrongfully charged you have a chance to dispute the fee by emailing a detailed account of the trip at support@drivequdos.com

**Please note that falsifying a mess can result in deactivation of your Qudos account**

ANTI-DISCRIMINATION POLICY

Discrimination occurs when a driver or rider discriminates against a person on the basis of ethnicity, color, sexual orientation, religion, gender, physical or mental disability, gender identity, marital status, and age. Discrimination is strictly prohibited and met with swift and drastic action that can lead to permanent deactivation on the app.

Discrimination may take many different forms. Qudos has a zero-tolerance policy and is dedicated to ensuring the safety and comfort for both rider and driver. Our aim is to provide the most welcoming and professional ride experience for both parties and we reinforce this policy under New York State Laws.

Qudos does not tolerate any kind of discrimination that creates a hostile and unpleasant environment for drivers and riders. Drivers or riders who are the source of discrimination will be terminated depending on the severity of their offence.

ANTI-HARASSMENT POLICY

We will not be lenient in cases of sexual harassment, assault, or violence, whether physical, emotional, or psychological. We will terminate riders and drivers who behave like this immediately! If you are the victim of harassment, assault, or both, please notify Qudos as soon as possible. We are responsible for hearing your claim, investigating the issue, and determining punishment.

We will investigate all claims discreetly. We will never disclose who made a complaint to anyone or give out information that may help others identify that person. We should all work towards preventing and addressing discrimination and harassment of any kind.

It is imperative that Qudos be made aware of all transgressions that occur. Depending on its severity and for the safety of our community the necessary route may be to get local authorities involved.

Be aware of your rights and speak up whenever you are harassed or discriminated against.

SMOKING POLICY

Smoking inside Qudos cars is prohibited due to potential passengers who have respiratory issues or may be bothered by the smell.

PET POLICY

Qudos permits licensed service animals to ride in vehicles without restrictions. Nonservice animals must confer with their driver after their ride request is accepted to find out if they’re okay with you bringing your animal. You can use the app to send a text message or call your driver.

As an additional courtesy, we kindly advise that you bring a blanket or carrier to reduce any messes such as shedding, hairballs, scratches, and etc.

Please help drivers keep their vehicles clean and fresh for all riders!

SEATBELT POLICY

It is the policy of Qudos that all drivers who operate vehicles wear properly fastened and adjusted seat belts for your safety. It is also highly advised that passengers wear seatbelts upon entering a vehicle. Qudos is dedicated to the safety of both their drivers and riders!

Drivers are advised to only facilitate as many passengers as there are seat belts in the car. For a large group please request two vehicles or a GRANDE to accompany everyone.

To guarantee safety for all please buckle up!

WEAPONS POLICY

Qudos prohibits both passengers and drivers from carrying a weapon during the course of the ride. This applies even if you are licensed to carry a handgun. Prohibited weapons include any form of weapon or explosive restricted under local, state, or federal regulation. This includes all firearms, illegal knives, or other weapons covered by the law. If you have a question about whether an item is considered a weapon under this policy, please contact us at support@drivequdos.com. You will be held responsible if it is a prohibited item.

Our goal is to ensure that everyone has a safe and enjoyable ride!

CHILD’S CAR SEATS

If you are planning on riding with children who require car seats, then you should bring a child seat or baby seat for them as the driver will not provide one. Infants must ride in a rear-facing baby seat, which must be fitted in the backseat.

PASSENGERS WHEELCHAIR ACCESSIBLE AND DISABILITIES

Passengers who require wheelchairs or scooters can request a ride in one of our wheelchair-accessible vehicles (WAV). WAV drivers specialize in safely transporting and assisting people with disabilities.

QUDOS PER MILE PER MINUTE POLICY

Powered by Google Maps API, the Qudos Map calculates the time and distance from the designated pickup location to the drop-off location providing the best route for our drivers.

Qudos combines Google Maps API time and distance as well as the mile per minute fee to determine the estimate for each ride. Please visit https://ridequdos.com/fares for the current rates.

All Calculations and estimates are done through Google Maps API. For further inquiries kindly contact us at support@drivequdos.com. Thank you! (All the rates are subject to change without prior notice.)
